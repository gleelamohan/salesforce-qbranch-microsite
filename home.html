On Demand

An experienced demo engineer can provide you with technical and product guidance to help you refine your story and select the right mix of Salesforce features to maximise your demo impact. As we are in the unique position of working on demos across a multitude of industries, geographies, and market segments we can help you tailor a rich experience that your customers will never forget.

Demo Components

Salesforce allows for extension and customisation using a variety of languages including our own native technologies such as APEX and Visualforce as well as Lightning and many javascript and CSS frameworks. Demo Engineers can help build bespoke demo components for your demos to highlight how we can drive customer success. We can help build demonstrations within the Lightning Experience, Console, Communities, Wave or Salesforce1. .

App Exchange

If you have identified product gaps that can be resolved by extending the product through custom development or if you have an app idea that you think is a market need, Demo Engineers can help you bridge the product gap or build the app ground up on the Salesforce platform. These apps and components can then be made available to all our customers through App Exchange. The Demo Engineer will own the development and release of the component or app on the App Exchange. .
